About Us

Welcome to Carebear, we design premium baby-care products designed with affordability, comfort and safety in mind.

Our Mission:

CareBear understands that parenthood is a journey filled with countless challenges and decisions. That is why we are committed to designing high quality baby-care essentials that will give you peace of mind by making life easier for you and your child. From clothing and toys to feeding products and hygiene products, every CareBear product is purposefully crafted to offer comfort, joy and safety.

  • Reliable customer support
  • Free shipping for purchases over $100 CAD
